Classic Vanilla Cake

A soft, fluffy, and delicious homemade vanilla cake perfect for any occasion

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Serving: 8

Difficulty: Easy

This is a simple and delicious vanilla cake recipe that's perfect for beginners.

Finished Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpos flour
  • 1 cup suger
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • tsp baking powder

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 180c [350f].
  2. Crem butter and suger until light and fluffy
  3. Add eggs one at a time and mix well
  4. Mix in flour and baking powder
  5. bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown

Tips

Do not overmixthe batter to keep the cake soft

You can add chocolate chips for extra flavor
