Simple Cake Recipe

A soft and delicious homemade cake perfect for any occasion.

Prep Time" 15mins

Cook Time: 30-40 mins

Servings: 6people

Difficulty:Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition. Stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour and baking powder, add to the creamed mixture and mix well. Finally, stir in the milk until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour or spoon the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven. Cake is done when it springs back to the touch.
